What I Do

In the realm of data, I dig deep into datasets using tools like Python and Machine Learning, unearthing patterns and essential insights. Recognizing connections, correlations, and meaningful trends is my daily routine. These discoveries aren’t kept in jargon; I simplify and showcase them visually so that everyone, from fellow scientists to stakeholders, can grasp their significance. With the aid of tools like Scikit-learn, I pick out the most relevant data points for creating predictions. My toolkit is vast, including Supervised ML and Neural Networks powered by TensorFlow. That means constant tinkering and experimenting to ensure the highest levels of accuracy and robustness in the predictions.
